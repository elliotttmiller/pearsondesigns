Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Minneapolis-St. Paul metropolitan area, including Wayzata, Edina, Minnetonka, and surrounding communities. For exceptional projects, we're happy to travel beyond our primary service area. We also offer virtual design services nationwide.

How much does interior design cost?

Every project is unique, so we create custom proposals based on your specific needs and budget. After your free consultation, you'll receive a transparent proposal with line-item pricing within 48 hours. No hidden fees, no design retainers‚Äîjust clear, upfront pricing.

What is your design process?

We start with a 30-minute virtual consultation on Zoom where we tour your space and brainstorm on a live Miro board. Then we create an interactive mood board you can comment on in real-time, followed by 3D previews and AR so you can see designs in your actual space. Once approved, we handle all procurement and installation.

Do you offer home staging?

Yes! We offer professional home staging, rental staging, and quick-turn styling services to help properties sell faster and for top dollar. Our strategic staging highlights your home's best features and creates an emotional connection with potential buyers.

How long does a project take?

Our average time from consultation to approved design is just 1.7 days. After approval, timeline depends on furniture availability and your schedule, but we work efficiently to get your space transformed as quickly as possible‚Äîoften in weeks, not months.
