Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Minneapolis-St. Paul metropolitan area, including Wayzata, Edina, Minnetonka, and surrounding communities. For exceptional projects, we're happy to travel beyond our primary service area.

How much does interior design cost?

Our interior design services are customized to each client's needs and budget. We offer both full-service design packages and hourly consultation rates. Contact us for a complimentary consultation to discuss your project and receive a detailed proposal.

What is your interior design process?

Our process begins with an initial consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle, and budget. We then create a comprehensive design plan, select furnishings and finishes, coordinate with contractors, and oversee installation to ensure every detail is perfect.

Do you offer home staging for real estate sales?

Yes, we specialize in professional home staging to help properties sell faster and for top dollar. Our strategic staging highlights your home's best features and creates an emotional connection with potential buyers.

Can I visit your showroom?

Absolutely! Our 3,000 sq ft showroom in Wayzata features carefully selected collections from top luxury furniture brands. We're open Monday through Saturday, 10am-5pm. Walk-ins are welcome, or schedule an appointment for personalized attention.

How long does a typical interior design project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A single room refresh may take 4-8 weeks, while a full-home renovation can take 3-6 months or longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation.

Ready to Transform Your Space?

Schedule a complimentary consultation to discuss your interior design or home staging project. We'll bring your vision to life with our expertise and attention to detail.
