Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our fast, collaborative design process.

Do I need to be tech-savvy?

No—we guide you through every tool. Most clients are comfortable in 5 minutes. If you can use Zoom, you can use our process. We'll walk you through Miro, 3D walkthroughs, and AR previews step by step.

How much does it cost?

Every project is unique. After your free consult, you'll receive a custom proposal with transparent pricing. We break down costs by scope, timeline, and deliverables—no hidden fees, no pressure. You'll know exactly what you're investing in before making any commitment.

How fast is your process?

Most clients receive their design proposal within 48 hours of consultation. Our average consultation-to-approval time is 1.7 days. We use smart tools like AI-assisted 3D modeling and collaborative mood boards to eliminate traditional delays without sacrificing quality.

Do you work remotely?

Yes! Our entire process is built for virtual collaboration. We work with clients nationwide through Zoom consultations, interactive Miro boards, and 3D walkthroughs. For local projects, we offer optional in-person installation support to ensure everything is perfect.

What if I want changes?

Your proposal includes revision rounds. Changes are easy to make during our collaborative workflow—simply comment on the Miro board or request adjustments during our Zoom reviews. Because you see everything in 3D before we order, major changes are rare. Most clients approve after 1-2 rounds of minor tweaks.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help. Book a free 30-minute consultation to discuss your project and get all your questions answered. No obligation—just clarity.
