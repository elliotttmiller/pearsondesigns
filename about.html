Our Story

About Pearson Designs

Creating sophisticated, timeless interiors for discerning homeowners in Minneapolis and Wayzata for over two decades.

Who We Are

Passion for Design, Commitment to Excellence

PEARSON Designs is a full-service interior design firm specializing in luxury residential projects throughout the Minneapolis-Wayzata area. Founded on the belief that exceptional design should be both beautiful and functional, we create spaces that reflect our clients' unique lifestyles while maintaining timeless elegance.

With over 20 years of experience, our team brings together expertise in interior architecture, furniture design, and spatial planning. We pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail, personalized service, and ability to seamlessly manage projects from initial concept through final installation.

Our Approach

Personalized Service

Every project begins with listening. We take the time to understand your vision, lifestyle, and aesthetic preferences to create spaces that are uniquely yours.

Timeless Design

We create interiors that transcend trends, blending classic elegance with contemporary sensibility for spaces that remain beautiful for years to come.

Exceptional Quality

From furniture selection to final styling, we source only the finest materials and work with trusted craftspeople to ensure impeccable results.
